We work our magic

Photography is our bread and butter. Our experienced photographers work with you to capture that "Thing" that makes your brand unique. All the while, taking the quality to a whole new level of excellence.

The first interaction that most people have with the car they purchase is often through the internet. As the old adage goes: "First impressions are everything". The first impression of any consumer when purchasing a car online is the photo paired with the car. Can you really risk leaving that opportunity to the luck of the draw? How many sales are you losing because customers overlook your inventory?

The finishing touch

Imagine you have two identical cars listed side by side. The 1st listing's photos are taken with a consumer grade digital camera, feature a lack of attention to detail, and an uncontrolled lighting environment. The car is clean, yet appears dirty, The paint doesn’t appear vibrant, and misrepresents the true color of the car. It's no wonder it doesn't leave a lasting impression. No steps have been taken to set it apart from its competition.

On the other hand, the 2nd listing's photos were taken with professional photography equipment, the photos feature well thought out shots which highlight the unique features of the car, and were taken in a controlled lighting environment with a soft overhead flash that brings out that cars distinctive physique. The shot not only stays true to its personality, it brings out the same emotional connection the viewer would have if they were standing in front of the car in person.
